# Telecom Sales One-Pager: Retention Agent Copilot

## Buyer Problem

Retention agents lose time and accuracy when they must manually inspect bills, discounts, device financing, internet bundles, expiry dates, and customer call context across slow internal systems.

Common failure points:

- Agents quote a price without including add-ons, device payments, taxes, roaming, or one-time charges.
- Promo expiry and bundle end dates are missed.
- Roaming or one-time charges are mistaken for regular monthly charges.
- Newer agents take longer to understand the account.
- Notes entered into internal systems are inconsistent.

## Product Promise

Retention Agent Copilot gives agents a source-backed billing and conversation workspace that helps them explain the bill, find retention risks, and produce clean after-call notes faster.

## Why Telecom Companies Should Care

- Lower average handle time by reducing bill investigation time.
- Improve first-call resolution by showing price drivers clearly.
- Reduce rework caused by inaccurate quotes or incomplete notes.
- Help new agents ramp faster with guided call-flow prompts.
- Improve compliance by keeping final offer verification in the official system.
- Create cleaner operational data through structured call summaries.

## Core Modules

- Bill Intelligence: PDF or system-fed bill parsing, original price, current before-tax monthly price, total after tax, discounts, expiry dates, add-ons, device payments, and contract end.
- Change Explanation: plain-language summary of why the bill changed, including promo expiry, HUP, roaming, one-time charges, or bundle changes.
- Agent Assist: live transcript-based suggested response, follow-up question, call stage, and safety reminders.
- Maestro Note Generator: copy-ready structured note with plan changes, pricing, and verification reminders.
- Audit Layer: source page references, missing-field warnings, and "verify before quote" guardrails.

## Suggested Pilot

Run a 4-week pilot with one retention team:

- Week 1: ingest sample bills and validate extraction accuracy.
- Week 2: run side-by-side agent workflow tests.
- Week 3: measure average handle time, quote accuracy, note quality, and agent satisfaction.
- Week 4: refine eligibility logic and prepare production integration plan.

## Measurable Outcomes

- Minutes saved per billing investigation.
- Percentage of calls with complete price explanation.
- Reduction in inaccurate quote escalations.
- Agent confidence score.
- Manager QA score on after-call notes.

## Important Positioning

This product should be sold as an agent copilot, not an autonomous sales bot. The agent remains responsible for final offer selection and must verify eligibility in the official system. That positioning is safer for telecom compliance and easier for operations leaders to adopt.

## Expansion Roadmap

- Maestro and CRM connector.
- Offer eligibility API connector.
- Real-time call transcription.
- Supervisor QA dashboard.
- Redaction and privacy guardrails.
- Multi-brand telecom bill parser.
- A/B testing by call type and agent tenure.
